About Us

We are a high energy physics research group in the physics department at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign. Our research interests include:
- The New Small Wheel upgrade of the ATLAS Detector
- Physics analysis of Higgs Boson decaying to multiple b-quarks and muons
- R & D for muon readout board for muon drift tube chambers (MDTs)
